When you request for a project, it follows following phases:
  1. Planning - It begins with disucssing aspects of the web design; its purpose, users & their information and information about stakeholders.
  2. Analysis - The information that is useful to the user is analyzed.
  3. Design & Development - The UI/UX (user interface & experience), the layout of the website i.e. wireframes, mockeup is designed and approved by you which is then coded into a website.
  4. Testing - The website is tested against the content, functions, information & navigation planned out in the earlier phases.
  5. Implementation & Maintenance - The website is launched successfully. Planning of how website and its content will be updated will also be decided in this phase.

Every idea is different and so is every project. Prices vary upon the days of work, functionality & features and complexity of project. So I try to charge per project a fee that is affordable.
